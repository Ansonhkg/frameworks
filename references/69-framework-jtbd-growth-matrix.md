# JTBD Growth Matrix

The JTBD Growth Matrix applies Jobs to Be Done thinking to growth. It helps teams identify the real task users are hiring the product to do.

## Why It Matters

Growth is not just about adding features or channels.

It often sits inside the job the user is trying to complete.

## The Matrix

The framework looks at jobs across multiple dimensions:

- Functional jobs
- Emotional jobs
- Social jobs
- Personal jobs

It helps teams connect user segments, opportunity areas, and growth actions.

## When to Use It

Use it when:

- Product growth stalls
- You want a new growth lever
- You need a deeper understanding of user behavior
- You want to refine value proposition and UX

## Intercom Case Study

Intercom evolved from a chat-oriented tool into a broader customer communication platform by expanding around the jobs customers needed done.

## How to Use It

1. Define the job type.
2. Define user segments.
3. Identify opportunity areas.
4. Prioritize them.
5. Turn the findings into an action plan.
